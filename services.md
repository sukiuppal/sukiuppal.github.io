## What I offer

### CBT Short-term therapy
Short-term therapy is anything between 12–20 CBT therapy sessions, depending on your needs.

### Psychodynamic Long-term therapy
Long-term therapy works within a time frame we agree on together — for example, 6 months to a year — or we can keep it open-ended, depending on what works best for you.

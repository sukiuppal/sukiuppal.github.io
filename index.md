## How it works

If you are unsure which type of therapy might be right for you, please don't hesitate to get in touch — I am happy to help you think through what could work best for your needs.

1. **Free 20-minute consultation call** — an opportunity to connect, ask any questions, and get a sense of whether I feel like the right fit for you.
2. **Two initial assessment sessions** — we explore the type of support you are looking for and agree on an approach and length of work that suits you best.
3. **Ongoing weekly sessions** — 50 minutes each, available both online and in person, in a space that is safe, open, and non-judgmental.

Everything you share is held in the strictest confidence.

### Approaches offered

- **Long-term / open-ended psychodynamic therapy** — for those wishing to explore things in depth
- **Short-term CBT** — focused, goal-oriented work
